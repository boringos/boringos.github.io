Do youth capsules exist?

Do youth capsules exist?

Did you find the Makropoulos remedy after all, or is it unrealistic? Found it! It turned out to be real.

For more than 2000 years, medicine has been using the leaves of one of the world's oldest trees for diseases of the brain and spinal cord. the decrease in blood circulation. But only now it has become clear that Ginkgo biloba leaves are nothing more than a magical means for prolonging youth, which dramatically slows down the aging process of cells.

When using Ginkgo, the brain's blood supply increases, which facilitates access to acetylcholine receptors in the hippocampus, improves the supply of oxygen to cells and the brain's utilization of glucose, reduces cognitive disorders and neurodegenerative age-related changes.

Indications for prescribing Ginkgo are many. The use of this "great" antioxidant improves:

· concentration and memory

* condition of vessels

· vision in people suffering from diabetes

· sexual potency

· condition in schizophrenia

* reduces depressive symptoms

* migraine attacks become less frequent

· lowers "bad" cholesterol

· vertigo is reduced

Recommended dose of Ginkgo: from 60 up to 180 mg per day of standardized leaf extract. It can also be found in tablets, liquid extracts, or anti-varicose gels. But precautions when using Ginkgo biloba, as well as any drug, no one canceled. It is not recommended for people, people with epilepsy or convulsions; those with heart rhythm disorders; those who suffer from bleeding or are taking anticoagulants (blood thinners); pregnant and lactating mothers. And it should also be remembered that Ginkgo can interact with some medications. Therefore, before taking it, you must consult your doctor!
